Flagship Projects

Signature initiatives engineered by the Interactors of Ananda College.

Community Service SPLISH SPLASH

Providing clean water infrastructure and structural renovations to schools lacking essential sanitary resources.

Community Service HOPE

Annual healthcare relief campaign offering essential medical equipment and direct infrastructure support to state clinics.

Club Service RHETORIK

The premier inter-school oratory championship creating massive professional communication pathways across the district.

Club Service INTERCON

Internal bonding games, sports encounters, and physical engagement training programs designed to unify member chemistry.

Green Life ECO ACTION

Massive beach cleaning, tree planting campaigns, and electronic waste processing drives building ecological sustainability.

International Understanding GLOBAL BOUNDS

Cultural exchange programs and collaborative digital community campaigns executed with global Twin Clubs.

Finance RACE FOR CHANGE

The signature walkathon and massive open carnival raising core funds to power the club's high-scale community operations.

Public Relations (PR) VOICE OF ANANDA

Digital media coverage campaigns and creative visual storytelling modules showcasing public youth action projects.
